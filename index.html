The Process

Discovery

All the processes below start with a detailed discovery session that higlights the problems that need to be solved.

Research & Analysis

We do research on the project so that we can approach the design from a user's perspective.

Design & Prototyping

The next step is wireframing and visual design then building interactive prototypes to test the flow and functionality.

Testing

This step is for identifying usability issues and gathering feedback to make necessary improvements to enhance user experience.

Sketching & Wireframing

Sketching and wireframing go hand in hand to set up a proper foundation for a user-friendly interface making web design effective.

Visual & Responsive Design

This is where the sketches and wireframes come to life. Colors, typography, images and graphics come together ro create the interface. Responsive design ensures that the website adapts according to screen size.

Development & Testing

The designs are turned into functional and interactive websites and testing ensures they are reliable.

Preparation & Assembly

We source and organize all the media to be used ie. raw footage, audio files, graphics etc. We then watch the footage then roughly select cuts.

Rough Cut & Fine Cut

Here we are able to get a skeleton of the sequence of events ensuring the narrative of the video flows as desired. Supporting footage(B-roll) is introduced to enhance the story.

Sound design and Special Effects

here we bring in the music, sound effects and graphic effects to help make the video more engaging for the viewer.

Concept Development & 3D Modelling

We start by defining a purpose then create a story board to outline key scenes and camera angles serving as a visual script. The next step is to create detailed 3D models for the product

Texturing, Animation & Lighting

We then apply textures to the 3D model to simulate real world surfaces. The animation process involves defining keyframes and movements then setting up virtual lights in the 3D environment.

Rendering & Post-Production

The last step is to render the animation sequences and combining them while adding elements like sound, music, text or special effects.
